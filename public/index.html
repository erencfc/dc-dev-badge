Hello! This website will help you to get your Active Developer Badge!

To get your badge, you have to create an Application first!

  • Go to Discord Developer Portal
  • Create a New Application
  • Go to the Bot tab
  • Click on Add Bot and then on Yes, do it!
  • Scroll down a little bit, and enable all the Privileged Gateway Intents then save it.

Once you have finished, click on the button below!

Now you have to add the bot to your discord server!

  • Create a new discord server
  • Invite your friend to the server (or invite your other acc)
  • Go to Discord Developer Portal again and click on your application
  • In General Information tab click on Copy under your Application ID
  • Put your Application ID in the input below and click on Submit
  • In the tab that opened, add your bot to your server that you just created.


Once you have finished, click on the button below!

Now you have to paste your bot's token in the input below!

  • Go to Discord Developer Portal again and click on your application then click on Bot tab
  • Click on Copy from the Token section to copy your bot's token (if you can't copy, Reset Token)
  • Paste your bot's token in the input below and click on Start

Bot is started successfully!

  • Now go to your server and use the /badge command with your friend/another account in your server to get your Active Developer Badge!
  • Your bot has been started and you have 5 minutes to use the bot! After that bot will be stopped automatically!
  • You can start the bot again or stop it by clicking on the buttons below!
